About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate developer who loves creating meaningful projects. I specialize in cloud computing, DevOps, and automation, leveraging my skills to build efficient and secure systems. I thrive on solving complex problems with elegant solutions, whether through scripting, cybersecurity, or AI-driven optimizations.

My journey began in 7th grade with a PLTW robotics class, where I discovered the excitement of bringing projects to life to solve real-world problems. That curiosity led me to dive deep into web development, object-oriented programming, and cloud technologies like AWS and Azure. Over time, I’ve expanded my expertise to include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, PowerShell, SQL, C#, C++, and UI/UX design.

Beyond technical skills, I bring strong leadership experience from student organizations and a commitment to continuous learning. I enjoy working in fast-paced environments, solving problems quickly, and assisting others with care. I'm always looking for new opportunities to apply my knowledge and grow in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
